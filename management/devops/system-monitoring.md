### Logging

#### Five Ws of logging:

* What happend?
* When did it happen?
* Where did it happen?
* Who was involved?
* Where did that entity come from?

#### Logging principles

1. Don't collect log data you won't use
2. Keep log data for as long as they can be used
3. Log only on what you must respond to
4. Don't exceed business security needs
5. Logs change
